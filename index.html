Role Overview

We are seeking an experienced English Communication Trainer to deliver offline corporate training programs for Customer Support teams. The role focuses on enhancing employees' communication quality, customer-handling skills, grammar accuracy, and overall service language proficiency.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct offline communication training for customer support executives across voice, email, and chat processes.
  • Train employees on professional communication, customer empathy, tone & clarity, neutral accent, and grammar usage.
  • Develop and deliver training modules on:
    • Spoken English
    • Business communication
    • Customer handling phrases & scripts
    • Call etiquette & soft skills
    • Email writing & chat etiquette
  • Evaluate learners through role plays, call simulations, mock chats/emails, and assessments.
  • Identify communication gaps and create customized training for new hires and existing employees.
  • Provide actionable feedback to improve language proficiency, customer interactions, and overall CSAT.
  • Track performance, maintain training reports, and coordinate with managers and QA teams for continuous improvement.
  • Create an engaging and professional classroom environment.

Preferred Skills

  • 4–7 years of experience as a Communication Trainer / English Trainer in BPO, Customer Support, or Corporate Training.
  • Strong command of spoken and written English with the ability to simplify concepts.
  • Experience in voice, email, or chat process training (preferred).
  • Excellent presentation, facilitation, and coaching skills.
  • Ability to handle diverse learners and drive measurable improvement.

Preferred Attributes

  • Certification in communication training, TEFL/TESOL, or soft skills (added advantage).
  • Experience working with customer support KPIs such as CSAT, quality scores, AHT impact, etc.
  • Strong understanding of customer service language, tone, and escalation handling.
