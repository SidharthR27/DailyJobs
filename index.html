Experience: 3–5 years

About the Role:

Manage and respond to security alerts, administer firewall/DLP/AV systems, and support enterprise cybersecurity controls and incident management.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage firewall rules, policies, updates
  • Monitor firewall logs for anomalies
  • Manage DLP tool policies & alerts
  • Ensure endpoint AV updates & compliance
  • Monitor privileged user activities (PAM/PIM)
  • Support SOC in incident investigations
  • Conduct security audits & prepare reports

Required Skills & Experience:

  • Hands-on with Palo Alto, Fortinet, Trend Micro
  • Understanding of SIEM, vulnerability management
  • Knowledge of firewall best practices
  • Good analytical & incident-response skills
