The candidate will identify new business opportunities, generate leads, promote cyber security services and training programs, and build strong relationships with clients to drive revenue growth for the cyber security domain.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify new business opportunities in Cyber Security services and consulting.
  • Generate leads through networking, cold calling, digital platforms, and industry events.
  • Promote cyber security solutions such as network security, vulnerability assessment, penetration testing.
  • Build strong relationships with corporate clients, educational institutions, and IT organizations.
  • Conduct product presentations and explain cyber security solutions to potential clients.
  • Prepare proposals, quotations, and business presentations.
  • Coordinate with the technical team to deliver customized cyber security solutions.
  • Follow up with leads and convert them into successful business deals.
  • Achieve monthly sales targets and contribute to overall business growth.
  • Maintain records of client interactions and sales pipeline.

Preferred Skills & Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, IT, Computer Science, or related field.
  • 1–3 years of experience in IT sales, cyber security sales, or technology solutions sales (Freshers with strong interest in cyber security sales can also apply).
  • Understanding of cyber security concepts and IT infrastructure is an advantage.
  • Strong communication, negotiation, and presentation skills.
  • Ability to work with sales targets and deadlines.

Key Skills

  • Lead Generation & Business Development
  • Cyber Security Solution Awareness
  • Client Relationship Management
  • Sales Strategy & Market Analysis
  • Negotiation & Closing Skills
